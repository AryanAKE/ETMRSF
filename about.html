About ETMRSF-2026

Bridging traditional knowledge with modern innovations for a sustainable world.

Conference Concept

The International Conference on Emerging Trends in Multidisciplinary Research for Sustainable Future (ETMRSF-2026) aims to provide a dynamic platform for sharing knowledge and fostering collaboration across diverse fields[cite: 5].

Focusing on emerging global and local challenges such as climate change, digital disruption, sustainable development, and inclusive education, the conference will bring together researchers, industry professionals, policymakers, and students.

"This conference aspires to inspire collaborative solutions for a more inclusive and sustainable world"[cite: 7].
The University

Established in 1994 by an Act of Parliament, Tezpur University offers interdisciplinary courses to meet regional aspirations and development needs of Assam.

The Centre (CMDR)

Formally established in 2020, CMDR enhances multidisciplinary education and research, building on the university's proven track record of collaborative authorship[cite: 14, 13].

Core Objectives

Share recent advances in multidisciplinary research among academicians and industry professionals[cite: 20].

Promote collaborative research across fields like Science, Technology, Social Sciences, and Management[cite: 21].

Encourage the application of multidisciplinary knowledge for solving real-world challenges[cite: 22].

Highlight the role of multidisciplinary research in sustainable development and innovation[cite: 23].
