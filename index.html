Institution Spotlight

Tezpur University
(A Central University)

Established by an Act of Parliament in 1994, Tezpur University stands as a premier seat of learning in the Northeast.

The objectives are to offer employment-oriented and interdisciplinary courses to meet regional aspirations and development needs of Assam.

TU aims to promote research in areas of special and direct relevance to the region and emerging areas of science and technology.

Organizing Secretariat

Centre for Multidisciplinary Research (CMDR)

Formally established in 2020, CMDR enhances education and research across traditional boundaries.

Legacy & Evolution

Tezpur University's research publications have a proven track record of multidisciplinary authorship. The center offers PhD and PG Open Elective courses. In 2023, the MoE awarded a Multidisciplinary IKS Centre titled 'Exploring Knowledge on Medieval Assam'.

Strategic Vision

Objectives of ETMRSF-2026

Share recent advances in multidisciplinary research among professionals and scholars.
